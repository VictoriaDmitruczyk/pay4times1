Our Mission and Values

Pay4times is a Fintech company offering a seamless Buy Now, Pay Later Option online and in-store. As a consumer, you get to shop your favorite products at your favorite stores by paying overtime in four equal installments.

Pay4times Buy-Now-Pay-Later solution helps customers to afford whatever they need, knowing they can pay later when able. Merchants who integrate this payment method into their system would therefore be able to attract more buyers, thereby generating more sales for their business.

Choosing Pay4times is choosing business growth.

Our goal is to empower consumers beyond their financial capacity, to enable people to acquire what they need when they need it without worrying about late payments and a deteriorating credit score. And while at it, we are helping businesses grow.
